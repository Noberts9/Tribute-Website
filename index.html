About J.J. Akinyi

Judith Jecinter Akinyi born on 29th September 1975 (29/09/1975).Is a medical practitioner licensed by Nursing Council of Kenya.

Life and Career

Judith is born into a family of four children. She is the second born daughter of Mrs. Hellen Atieno, sister to Michael ,Fredrick and Pacifica.
She studied at Bhaghini school for her primary education then proceeded to Nyabisawa Girls High school she did her A-levels ,she later joined KEnya Medical Training College to pursue her dream career of being a nurse which was inspired by her mother.
She finished her diploma course and went for a post graduate diploma in Hiv/aids peaditric n adolescent champion.
Judith is married to George Too, with five children.

work

Judith after finish her course and graduating from college got licensed and stated working at government hospital in Siaya county.
But she knew she was destined for greatness and she took a leap of faith and looked for a job and landed one with an NGO company called Catholic Medical Mission Board (CMMB) as a programme officer as a Hiv/aids peaditric n adolescent.
Her achievements are ;
  • Focal person in developing Hiv/aids work place policy.
  • Key play in PMTCT.
  • Involve in Occupational health safety activities
She is currently a key player in cervical cancer screening and treatment.
